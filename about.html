About Me.

Hi, my name is Trevor.

Through this experience, I was able to develop a deep skill set with computer science that I used to create new efficient solutions resulting in 85% increase in the stores net promoter score. That role inspired me to learn more about Full stack software engineering and I really became passionate about technology as a whole. I was able to solve the client’s problem and deliver a solution while saving the company valuable resources.
I’m looking to launch a career in software engineering and work in a role with an innovative team working to solve the company’s needs. This will give me the opportunity to learn and grow in my field.
